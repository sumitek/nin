Why do Nursing from Nightingale Institute of Nursing

Nightingale Institute of Nursing, Noida is well established pioneer Institute in healthcare education which constantly redesigns the way healthcare professionals are trained. As nursing continues to be a strong career option, we have comprehensive campus programmes for nursing education aspirants: M.Sc. Nursing (Obstetric & Gynecology, Medical Surgical, Mental Health, Child Health, Community Health), B.Sc. Nursing, Post Basic B.Sc. Nursing and GNM. Nightingale Institute of Nursing provides health care programmes that are innovative, clinical relevant, and technology infused.

We provide huge platform for personality development and effective communication.

Hospitals like Fortis, Medcity Medanta, Max Healthcare, Apollo, Sitaram Bhartia, Pushpanjali Crossley, Dharamshila Cancer Hospital approach us for campus selection of our students.

If any candidate is determined to pursue a meaningful career and bring a difference in people's lives, there is no more vibrant place than Nightingale Institute of Nursing, Noida.
