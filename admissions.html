Admissions

Admission Procedure

The admission form completed in all respects, together with necessary documents should be submitted to the admission section.

General Guidelines

The academic calendar of the Institution is normally from September to September for GNM Course. For B.Sc. Nursing from July to July as per University Norm.

List of Documents for Admission

GNM/B.Sc. (N) M.Sc. (N)
10th Mark sheet and passing certificate
12th Mark sheet and passing certificate
Medical Fitness Certificate
Character /Conduct Certificate
Migration Certificate
Transfer Certificate
Any other certificate 		10th mark sheet
12th mark sheet
B.Sc. (N) mark sheet (4th year)
B.Sc. (N) certificate
Experience certificate
RN/RM certificate
Relieving order
P.B.B.Sc. (N): All above documents along with GNM mark sheet, certificate and experience certificate.

 

Rejection of Application Form

The Application Form may be rejected due to any of the following reasons:

  • The candidate fails to fulfill the eligibility conditions.
  • The fee is not enclosed.
  • The application form is not signed by the candidate and/or his/her parent./ guardian.
