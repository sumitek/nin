Anti-Ragging Policy

Admissions

  • We offer B.sc Nursing programme only for female candidates. Hence male candidates need not apply for B.Sc (N).
  • We are open for both male & female candidates in GNM, P.B.B.Sc (N) & M.Sc (N).
  • Male & female candidates can apply for integrated nursing programme of GNM & P.B.B.Sc of total 5 years.
  • For M.sc (N) Candidates its mandatory to mention at least two specialization of interest in their application.

Admission Procedure

Application form for admission entrance test/interview is available on this website. Please click the button ("ONLINE APPLICATION FOR ADMISSION OPEN") for particular course and make sure you fill the form completely.

Eligibility Criteria for Admission in any Nursing Programmes

Candidate must:

  • Qualify Entrance exam and interview scheduled as per Institution policy.
  • Meet the norms given by Indian Nusing Council/Ch. Charan Singh University/U.P. Nurses and Midwives Council.
  • Filling online application form is only a gateway for entrance exams.
  • No candidate can claim admission only by filling the online application form.

 

Details Required for Application Form

After submission of the online application form, the candidate must send original scanned copies (only by CamScanner, no photos accepted) of the following certificates via email to helpdesknin@gmail.com

  • 10th MarkSheet
  • 10th Certificate
  • 12th MarkSheet
  • 12th Certificate
  • Candidates applying for P.B.B.Sc.(N) should also send
    Scanned copy of GNM I, II, III year, Internship, RN/RM certificate and diploma certificate.
  • Candidates applying for M.Sc.(N) should also send
    Scanned copy of B.Sc.(N) (I to IV year) / P.B.B.Sc(N) marksheets, degree, RN/RM certificate and experience certificates.

Candidates applying for all programs should also send the following:

  • Scanned copy of Aadhaar Card
  • Latest Passport Photo
  • Scanned Formal Signature
  • Caste certificate (if applicable)
  • Scanned copy of Mother and Father Aadhar card (only for purpose of name matching with 10th marksheet)

Rejection of Application Form

The Application Form may be rejected due to any of the following reasons:

  • The candidate fails to fulfill the eligibility conditions.
  • After submitting the application form scanned copies of all documents are not sent in mail immediately.
  • Candidate fails to appear for the online test and interview on the given date and time by the institute.

 

Rejection of Admission

The Admission may be canceled in any of the following conditions:

  • The candidate fails to present the original certificates at the time of admission.
  • The Total Fee amount of first instalment is not deposited at given date and time.
  • The university is the sole authority to reject the admission if any discrepency is found in any document.
