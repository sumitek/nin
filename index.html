Late Shri Suresh Chand Jain, the Patron, envisioned Nightingale Institute of Nursing 69 years ago. Nightingale Institute of Nursing is run by Nightingale Education Society under the presidentship of Mr. Ashok Jain. GNM was started in September, 2003 with 18 students and B.Sc.
